# SKYLearn-Innovation — Learner Projects

**Community learner portfolio built under the mentorship of Dingaan Mahlatse Machethe**

SKYLearn-Innovation is a registered South African NPO providing free STEM and coding education to community learners. This repository showcases data science and ML projects built by top-performing learners.

---

## Projects

### 📊 [ZA-STEM Insights](./za-stem-insights/)
**South African Matric Performance Analyser**

Built by 5 SKYLearn-Innovation learners. Analyses 10 years of SA NSC matric results across 9 provinces and 10 subjects. Includes a RandomForest at-risk predictor and an interactive Streamlit dashboard.

**Stack:** Python · Pandas · scikit-learn · Streamlit · Plotly
